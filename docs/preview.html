WHAT IS IT?

Future Sound of Bristol is a grant program to help fund music-related creative endeavours for emerging artists and labels in and around Bristol, UK.

Organised by Audiotarky, a Bristol-based ethical music streaming platform, and Factory Studios, a creative space in the heart of Bristol.

WHY?

The Pandemic and the Cost of Living Crisis have had a significant impact on artists and labels, making it difficult for them to pursue their craft.

Future Sound of Bristol aims to provide financial support to these music creatives and showcase the exceptional musical talent that the city has to offer.

Prizes

1st Prize

£5k + 1 month membership at Factory Studios

2nd Prize

£2k + 6 sessions at Factory Studios

3rd Prize

Three prizes of £1k + 3 sessions at Factory Studios

Access to Factory Studios available 7 days a week. Each session is 3 hours long and for rehearsal only. Sessions to be used off-peak.

Who can enter?

Although the competition is centered in Bristol, UK-based independent musicians and labels from all genres are encouraged to participate

How will it work?

Entrants have to:

  • Sign up to Audiotarky and us know about your project & your plans for how you'll use your prize.
  • Upload music to Audiotarky and tell us your favourite track.
  • Share your Audiotarky profile on social media using the #futuresoundofbristol and encourage people to listen to your tracks!

We want to hear a broad range of music, representing the diversity and vibrancy of Bristol. We hope you to feel empowered to apply and particularly welcome applications from people who are currently under represented in the music industry.

The top 25 artists and labels will be selected based on social media engagement and total streaming time of their music on Audiotarky during the competition period.

A panel of guest judges, including Elder Island’s David Havard and award-winning Record Producer Ali Chant, will then pick the five winners based on artist / label merit and their proposed plans for the prize money.

Timeline

  • 13th Feb > 10th April: Applications open
  • 11th April > 20th April: Judging period
  • 21st April: Winners announced
